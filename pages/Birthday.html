Birthday Parties

Have your birthday party at Champion Gymnastics!

Whether you have a princess or super hero, we have the party place for you!

Come have FUN in our 20,000 square foot gym equipped with trampolines, foam pit, and much more!

Each party has 55 minutes of gym time, 50 minutes of party time, and 15 minutes of clean up time.

What's Provided?

  • Party space
  • Tables
  • Table Cloths
  • Chairs
  • Balloons
  • Plates, Utensils
  • Clean Up

Let the kids have fun and let out coaches do the work!

Cost for 12 children: Members $225.00 / Non-members: $250.00 Each additional child: $10.00.

Party times: Saturday 12:30 & 3:30. Sunday 11:00 & 2:00

Please call (209) 477-8978 to book your child’s party today!

50% deposit due at booking.

Total number of children attending needs to be communicated no later than Wednesday before the party in order to staff appropriately.

2 hour time limit for each party.

$15 charge for each additional 15 minutes the party exceeds 2 hours. Each participant must complete a waiver in order to participate. No adults allowed on the equipment or foam pit.
