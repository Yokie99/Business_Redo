OUR CLASSES
Preschool Classes
Parent Tot  
- 45 min, Once a week
$67/month
For boys and girls ages 18 months to 3 years old. Playful parent and tot class centered around age appropriate developmental movements. Parents will assist children with skills and on the equipment with the aide of the instructor.
Tiny Tot  
- 45 min, Once a week
$67/month
Emphasizes fundamental gymnastics positions and skills on all gymnastics apparatus, while helping to develop focus, strength, and flexibility. The emphasis of our professionally developed pre-school curriculum is on safety and FUN while learning gymnastics and movement skills.
Girl’s Recreational Classes
Level 1  
- 55 min, Once a week (Ages 5+)
$89/month
We offer beginning gymnastics classes for the school aged child either just beginning gymnastics or those who may be a little rusty. These students will advance through the fundamental skill progression of gymnastics on all of the Olympic events. Classes are separated by age. Call for class times.
Level 2  
- 55 min, Once a week (Ages 5+)
$89/month
This class is for those who have completed our beginner program or for those who have some gymnastics experience. Our intermediate level gymnasts will further develop their strength, flexibility and skills on each event. Classes are designed to provide a more challenging environment focusing on their gymnastics skills while continuing to promote their self-esteem and body awareness.*Students must be evaluated to register for this class.
Level 3  
- 55 min, Once a week (Ages 5+)
$129/month
This class is intended for gymnasts who have either completed our intermediate program, or who have gymnastics experience. Our curriculum is designed to provide a multitude of challenges to enhance gymnastics abilities.

Tumbling
Beginning Tumbling  
- 55 min, Once a week
$89/month
This is a beginner level class for tumblers with little to no experience. Novice tumblers will learn basic tumbling progressions in order to build a strong foundation for learning more difficult skills including but not limited to: forward and backward rolls, handstands, hurdles, cartwheels, bridges, fly backs, and back bends.
Intermediate Tumbling  
- 55 min, Once a week
$89/month
This class is for gymnasts, cheerleaders and tumblers with experience or who have successfully completed our beginning tumbling program. Skills that will be developed in this class include but are not limited to: Round offs, Back bend/bridge kick over, back walk-over, Front walk over, front handspring, power hurdles.
Advanced Tumbling  
- 55 min, Once a week
$89/month
This class is for gymnasts, cheerleaders and tumblers with experience or who have successfully completed our intermediate tumbling program. Skills that will be developed in this class include but are not limited to: Round off back handsprings, aerials, standing tucks, punch fronts.
