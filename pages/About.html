CHAMPION'S BACKGROUND



History
Previously Lescisin Gymnastics Academy, Champion Gymnastics is now owned by one of Lescisin's former gymnasts, Jennifer Shipman- Krause. Jennifer has been involved in the sport of gymnastics for over 40 years. She competed nationally in the Junior Olympic Program as well as for San Jose State University. Jennifer's extensive involvement in gymnastics including holding a Brevet Judge's Rating and acting as the National Representative for USA Gymnastics. Along with our excellent staff of gymnastics professionals, Chuck Krause is manager of Champion Gymnastics. Chuck has extensive experience as well including involvement with several nationally recognized programs. His enthusiasm, energy, and innovative ideas are unparalleled.
Our Mission / Vision
Champion's mission is to provide excellent service in a fun, safe, and supportive learning environment. Our staff strives to teach life lessons of hard work, dedication, integrity, and sportsmanship through gymnastics. Our ultimate goal is to guide our students be better every day and to continue their growth each time they enter Champion Gymnastics Academy. We do out best to provide the tools for each student to make their dreams come true.
